3. How Compass Pro Works

Compass Pro operates entirely on your device with zero data transmission to servers:

3.1 Sensor Data (Magnetometer & Accelerometer)

  • What it does: Reads your device's compass and motion sensors to determine direction
  • Where it goes: Processed locally on your device only
  • Storage: Not stored anywhere - used in real-time only
  • Transmission: NEVER transmitted off your device

3.2 Location Data (GPS)

  • Purpose: To display your coordinates and calculate true north (magnetic declination correction)
  • Processing: Calculated entirely on your device
  • Storage: Not stored permanently - only while app is active
  • Transmission: NEVER sent to any server or third party
  • Optional: You can deny location permission and the app still works (magnetic north mode only)

3.3 App Settings & Preferences

  • What we store: Your theme preference (light/dark), measurement units, north type (magnetic/true)
  • Where: Stored locally on your device using Android's secure storage
  • Access: Only accessible by Compass Pro on your device
  • Transmission: Never leaves your device

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google AdMob (Advertising)

This app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process certain information:

Information AdMob May Collect:

  • Advertising ID (a unique, resettable ID for advertising purposes)
  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • IP address (for general location - city/country level only)
  • App interaction data (which ads you view or click)

Important: This data is collected by Google, not by us. We do not have access to this information. Google's privacy policy governs how they use this data.

Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

How to Control Ad Personalization:

  1. Opt Out of Personalized Ads (Android): Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  2. Reset Advertising ID (Android): Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID
  3. Remove Ads Completely: Purchase the ad-free version for $0.99 to remove all advertisements

4.2 Google Play Billing

If you purchase the ad-free version, the transaction is securely processed by Google Play's billing system:

  • We do NOT have access to your payment information (credit card, billing address, etc.)
  • Google handles all payment processing securely
  • We only receive confirmation that the purchase was successful
  • Your purchase status is stored locally on your device

Google Play's privacy policy applies to billing transactions.

5. Permissions Explained

The app requests the following permissions. Here's exactly why and how each is used:

ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION

  • Why: To display your coordinates and calculate true north (magnetic declination varies by location)
  • How it's used: GPS coordinates are read from your device, processed locally, and displayed to you
  • Transmission: Never transmitted anywhere
  • Optional: You can deny this permission. The compass will still work using magnetic north

INTERNET

  • Why: Required to display advertisements from Google AdMob
  • How it's used: Only used by AdMob to download and show ads
  • Note: No other data is transmitted. The app works offline except for ads

ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE

  • Why: To check if internet is available before attempting to load ads
  • How it's used: Prevents errors when trying to load ads without internet
